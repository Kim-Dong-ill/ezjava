I Knew
if I stayed around long enough,
something like this
would happen.
This is the true joy in life,
the being used for a purpose recognized by yourself
as a mighty one instead of a feverish selfish
little clod of ailments and grievances
complaining that the world will not devote itself
to making you happy.
I want to be thoroughly used up
when I die, for the harder I work the more I live.
I rejoice in life for its own sake.
